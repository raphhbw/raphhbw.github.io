Projects

Automated Search for Spectroscopic Exocomet Transits (in prep.)
Raphaël Bendahan-West, Grant Kennedy, Paul Strøm, David Brown - University of Warwick

Pipeline searching for variable absoprtion features in ionised Calcium (CaII K line) -ie. signs of exocomet transits, in the entire HARPS archive.

Self-Organising Map (Summer project)
Raphaël Bendahan-West, Grant Kennedy, Paul Strøm, David Brown - University of Warwick

Use of an unsupervised Machine Learning classification algorithm called a Self-Organising Map (SOM) to classify stellar spectra according to their different shapes. This was an attempt to reduce the number of false positive detections in our search for exocomet transits -eg. all stars showing highly variable stellar activity (known false positive detections of our exocomet search) would be classified together, providing an easier method to identify and filter them out.
