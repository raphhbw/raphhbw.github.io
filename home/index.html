Raphaël Bendahan-West

I am a MSc by Research graduate from the Astronomy and Astrophysics group at the University of Warwick. I was working under the supervsion of Grant Kennedy, David Brown, and Paul Strøm in the Dics Research Group.

My research is mainly focused on exocomet detection using spectroscopy. As part of my MSc, I have developed an automated algorithm that searches for exocomet transits in the High Accuracy Radial velocity Planet Searcher (HARPS) archive.
